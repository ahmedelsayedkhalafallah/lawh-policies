Privacy Policy for Lawh Applications

Last Updated: October 24, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Lawh (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our mobile applications: Lawh (Student) (com.lawhacademy.student) and Lawh Staff (com.lawhacademy.staff) (collectively, the “Services”).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password you provide when you create an account (e.g., via Firebase Authentication, including Google Sign-In).
  • User-Generated Content: Profile pictures, files you upload, messages, and other content you create within the apps (e.g., via Firebase Storage).
  • Usage and Device Data: Information about how you use our Services, your device model, operating system, and unique device identifiers (e.g., Firebase Cloud Messaging token for push notifications).
  • Payment Information: If you make a purchase, payment details are collected and processed by our third-party payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal). We do not store your full credit card information.
  • Media and Hardware Access (with your permission):
    • Camera (NSCameraUsageDescription): To allow you to take photos for your profile or for lessons.
    • Photo Library (NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription): To allow you to upload photos from your device.
    • Microphone (NSMicrophoneUsageDescription): To enable audio features during live sessions or lessons (e.g., via Agora).
    • Bluetooth (NSBluetoothAlwaysUsageDescription): To connect to peripheral devices related to our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our Services.
  • Authenticate users and secure your account.
  • Process transactions.
  • Enable communication between users (e.g., student and tutor).
  • Send you push notifications, with your consent.
  • Improve and personalize our Services.

3. Information Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with the following third parties:

  • Google (Firebase): We use Firebase for backend services, including authentication, database (Firestore), file storage, push notifications, and analytics.
  • Agora.io, Inc.: We may use Agora to facilitate real-time audio and video communication between users.
  • Payment Processors: To process payments securely.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Deletion

You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time. For instructions on how to do this, please see our Data Deletion Instructions Page.
